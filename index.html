Why use a travel credit card?

1. Save on flights & hotels

Earn air miles and hotel points that convert into free flights, upgrades and nights.

2. Lounge access

Escape crowded airports with free or discounted lounge entry.

3. Built-in travel insurance

Many cards include delay protection, luggage coverage and cancellation insurance.

Travel card FAQ

Is lounge access worth it?

If you fly 4–5+ times per year, lounge access often pays for itself in food, comfort and quiet workspaces.

Which card gives the most air miles?

Premium and airline-partnered cards usually provide the highest multipliers on flight purchases.
